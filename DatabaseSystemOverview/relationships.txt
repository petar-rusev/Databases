Types relationships are One-to-many (or many-to-one),Many-to-many, One-to-one.
Relationship one-to-many – used often.
Single record in one table has many corresponding records in the other table.
Relationship many-to-many
Records in the one table have many corresponding records in the other table and vice versa
Relationship one-to-one
A single record in one table corresponds to a single record in the other table
Used to model inheritance between tables
